RECIPE!

  • RecipeTin Eats' Easy Chocolate Brownies: Uses 200g dark chocolate chips and butter melted together, mixed with brown sugar and eggs for a moist, rich result; bake for 24 to 28 minutes depending on desired gooiness.
  • Sally's Baking Addiction's Seriously Fudgy Brownies: Combines cocoa powder, melted chocolate, and chocolate chips for a triple-chocolate experience; requires beating sugar and eggs for 3 minutes until pale and ribbon-like before adding dry ingredients.
  • Blue Bowl Recipes' 5-Ingredient Brownies: A simple mix of butter, sugar, eggs, flour, and cocoa powder; beat sugar and eggs on high speed for 3 to 4 minutes to ensure a crackly top, then bake for 33 to 38 minutes.
  • Gimme Delicious' One Bowl Cocoa Brownies: The quickest option, mixing melted butter, sugar, cocoa, eggs, and flour in a single bowl; bake for approximately 20 minutes to keep them super fudgy.

    • WE can also use coco powder, coconut powder, whip cream on top for extra flavour!
